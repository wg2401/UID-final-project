Aphrodite
Goddess of Love and Beauty
Overview
💫Goddess of love and beauty
💫Represents attraction and desire
💫Influences relationships between gods and humans
💫Central to many myths involving romance
Personality
✨Charismatic and influential
✨Can cause both love and conflict
✨Sometimes manipulative in myths
Symbols
🕊️Dove — represents love and harmony
🌹Rose — symbolizes beauty and passion
🪞Mirror — represents self-image and attraction
