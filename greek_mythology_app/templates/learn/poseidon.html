Poseidon
God of the Sea and Earthquakes
Overview
💫God of the sea, storms, and earthquakes
💫Brother of Zeus and Hades
💫Ruler of all oceans and waterways
💫Highly important to sailors and seafarers
Personality
✨Powerful but unpredictable
✨Can be calm or violently destructive
✨Known to cause storms and quakes when angered
Symbols
🔱Trident — his iconic weapon and symbol of power
🐴Horse — said to have created the first horse
🌊Waves — emblem of his domain over the seas
