Relationships
The Divine Family Tree
Overview
💫 Gods are part of an interconnected family
💫 Most relationships trace back to Zeus
💫 Family bonds explain many myths and conflicts
Origins — The Titans
🌍 The Titans ruled before the Olympian gods
👑 Cronus was the leader of the Titans and father of Zeus, Poseidon, and Hades
⚡ Zeus overthrew Cronus to become king of the gods
Power Division
⚡ Zeus — ruler of the Sky
🔱 Poseidon — ruler of the Sea
💀 Hades — ruler of the Underworld
Children of Zeus
🦉 Athena — born from Zeus's head, mother was Metis
🌞 Apollo — god of the sun and music
🏹 Artemis — goddess of the hunt, twin sister of Apollo
Conflicts
⚔️ Family tensions frequently cause rivalries among the gods
⚔️ Athena represents strategic warfare and wisdom — the opposite of brute force and chaos
⚔️ Zeus and Poseidon often compete for influence over gods and mortals
