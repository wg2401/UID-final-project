Athena
Goddess of Wisdom and War Strategy
Overview
💫 Goddess of wisdom, warfare strategy, and crafts
💫 Born fully armored from the head of Zeus
💫 Patron goddess of the city of Athens
💫 Champion of heroes like Odysseus and Perseus
Personality
✨ Calm, strategic, and highly intelligent
✨ Favors reason and diplomacy over brute force
✨ Fierce protector of justice and civilization
Symbols
🦉 Owl — represents wisdom and keen sight
🫒 Olive tree — her gift to Athens, symbol of peace
🛡️ Aegis shield — emblem of divine protection
