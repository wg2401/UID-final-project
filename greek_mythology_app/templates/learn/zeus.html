Zeus

Overview
💫King of the Olympian gods
💫Ruler of the sky and weather
💫Controls lightning and thunder
💫Most powerful of all the gods
Personality
✨Authoritative and dominant leader
✨Associated with justice and order
✨Decisive but impulsive
✨Often involved in conflicts with other gods
Symbols
⚡Thunderbolt — his weapon and ultimate symbol of power
🦅Eagle — represents his authority over the skies
